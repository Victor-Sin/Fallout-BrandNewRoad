After a long period of conflict, in 2077, a nuclear war broke out. Following decades of ruin, survive in a shattered world and write your destiny.

1969

After the end of World War II in 1945, due to the rising of communism, the USA decided to create thirteen Commonwealths to prevent a political transition of their states. Consequently, the national flag has been redesigned to represent the Commonwealths and the unity of the country. Furthermore, a long period of scarcity of resources began, particularly oil, therefore some countries like the USA embraced nuclear energy to improve their weaponry.

In 2052, oil reserves begin to run dry and Resource Wars begin across the world. In first, Mexico has been invaded in 2051 by the USA to enhance, insignificantly, their production of oil. Next year, the European Commonwealths started a campaign to conquest the Middle East and acquire their resources. At the same period, New Plagues arise in the U.S. and kill more than 200 000 people, a national lockdown has been declared at the end of the year.

China seeing the last oil reserves dry, tried to launch two operations to prevent their collapse. The first is to build an oil platform on the Pacific, the second is to trade talks with the USA to export their oil. However, the U.S. refused to export its resources and sabotaged the oil platform project of China. In winter, China invades Alaska and takes control of the last oil reserve. In answer, powers armor is sent to the Anchorage Front Line. However, Canada has considerable reticence to allow American troops on their soil.

Canada’s resources depleted by U.S. Army cause a lot of riots and protestations. Finally, an attack occurs on the Alaskan pipeline. In retaliation, the U.S. government decided to annex Canada by force in 2072. Two years later, the U.S. Army is deployed in China. Due to the long supply lines, the United States can no longer move forward over the Yangtze River. Furthermore, sino-Americans are sent to a concentration camp in the southwest of the U.S. and are used for military experiments.

In 2075, a new project lead by Wesk Tek is born, his objective is to cure the New Plague but it evolves into a virus to create super soldiers by increasing size and resistance to viral contagions. On January 10th, 2077, Alaska has been retaken by the American forces. A few months later, the October 23rd the Great War begins. The world seeing Chinese and American nuclear bombs deploy, each country deploys their own, and an exchange of fire ensued. No one knows who strikes first. In less than 2 hours, nuclear warheads strike the entire world.
